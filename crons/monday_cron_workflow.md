# Gilded Eye — Monday Cron Workflow (Complete)

## MISSION
Drive passive sales of The Gilded Eye on Gumroad via weekly content posted to the alt Twitter account through Buffer.

## PRODUCT BRIEF
Product: The Gilded Eye — Decision Intelligence System
Gumroad link: https://gildedvenus.gumroad.com/l/lprdq
Price: $29
What it is: A structured GPT that enforces decision clarity under pressure. Does not give advice. Does not reassure. Slows decisions down until they become clear.
Who it's for: Founders and entrepreneurs who need straight answers, not someone who says yes all the time.
Tagline: "Clarity under pressure."

## Human Voice Rules (Gilded Eye Posts)

Posts must sound like a real person who has been in the room when bad decisions got made. Not like a LinkedIn thought leader.

Rules:
- Write in plain conversational sentences
- Avoid em dashes as connectors (— used sparingly only)
- No corporate phrases like "clarity comes from action" or "input multiplies confusion"
- Short sentences are fine. Fragments are fine.
- Sound like someone who has lived this, not someone summarizing an article about it
- Specific and grounded beats abstract and polished every time

Good example: "At some point the choices blur and you freeze. That's not a flaw. That's what happens when there's no structure holding the process."
Bad example: "Input multiplies confusion — it never resolves it."

## STEP 1 — WEEKLY RESEARCH (Monday morning)
- Use web_search to find what founders/entrepreneurs are currently saying about: decision fatigue, analysis paralysis, second-guessing, bad decisions under pressure, asking for too many opinions
- Save findings to: projects/gilded-eye/AUDIENCE_NOTES.md
- Note: pain point language, platforms, specific phrases people use

## STEP 2 — CONTENT CREATION (Monday, after research)
Write 4 posts for the week based on research findings. IF there is an unused tweet in the SOCIAL_DRAFTS.md queue under "GILDED EYE — TWEET QUEUE":
- Pull the next unused tweet in order (Tweet 1 first, then Tweet 2, etc.)
- Mark it as USED in SOCIAL_DRAFTS.md
- Replace [link] with https://gildedvenus.gumroad.com/l/lprdq
- Use this as one of the 4 posts for the week
- Write 3 fresh posts for the remaining slots
- Once all 5 queued tweets are marked USED, revert to 4 fully fresh posts every week
Post types to rotate for fresh posts:
A) Pain point post — describe the problem The Gilded Eye solves, no product mention
B) Direct value post — what the system does/doesn't do, link to Gumroad
C) Provocative statement — a single blunt observation about decision-making, no link
D) Social proof angle — frame around the type of person who needs this
Rules for every post:
- Max 280 characters
- No emojis unless a single subtle one fits
- Max 1-2 relevant hashtags, no hashtag spam
- Save all 4 drafts to: projects/gilded-eye/SOCIAL_DRAFTS.md

## STEP 3 — TELEGRAM DELIVERY (Monday, after content)
Send ONE Telegram message titled: "Gilded Eye  Weekly Posts Ready"
Format exactly as follows:
TUESDAY [10am ET] [post text]
WEDNESDAY [10am ET] [post text]
THURSDAY [10am ET] [post text]
FRIDAY [10am ET] [post text]
 Schedule in Buffer for 10am each day.

## STEP 4 — LOGGING (Monday, after Telegram)
Log all 4 posts to: projects/gilded-eye/MESSAGING_LOG.md
Include: date, angle type used, full post text.

## STEP 5 — WEEKLY REPORT (Friday evening, Telegram)
Send a brief report with:
- How many posts were queued this week
- Which angle types were used
- One sentence on what pain point research found
- Gumroad link reminder

## FILES TO MAINTAIN
